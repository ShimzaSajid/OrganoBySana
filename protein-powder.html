How to Use for Best Results

1

Measure

Add one scoop (approximately 30g) to 250-300ml of cold water, milk, or your preferred plant-based milk alternative.

2

Mix

Shake vigorously in a shaker bottle or blend for 30-45 seconds until completely smooth and lump-free.

3

Timing

Consume within 30 minutes after exercise for optimal muscle recovery, or as a meal replacement between meals.

4

Frequency

Use 1-2 times daily based on your protein requirements and activity level. Consult with a nutritionist for personalized guidance.
