How to Use for Best Results

1

Application

Take a small amount of oil (about 2-3 teaspoons for medium length hair) and gently massage into scalp using fingertips in circular motions for 5-10 minutes.

2

Distribution

Work the oil through the length of your hair, focusing on ends and damaged areas. Use a wide-tooth comb to ensure even distribution.

3

Duration & Frequency

Leave for at least 2 hours or overnight for best results. Use 2-3 times per week for optimal hair health improvement.

4

Washing

Wash with a mild, sulfate-free shampoo. You may need to shampoo twice to remove all oil residue completely.
